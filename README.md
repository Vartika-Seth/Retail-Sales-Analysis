# 📊 Retail Sales & Profitability Analysis (Power BI)

An end-to-end Power BI project focused on analyzing retail sales performance and uncovering the gap between **revenue growth and profitability** using the Superstore dataset.

---

## 🚀 Project Objective

To identify key business drivers affecting **sales, profit, and margins**, and provide actionable insights to improve overall profitability.

---

## 🧰 Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Excel / CSV Dataset

---

## 📌 Key Metrics

* **Total Sales:** 2.30M
* **Total Profit:** 286.40K
* **Profit Margin:** 14.22%

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 🌍 Regional Performance

* West region generates the highest sales, followed by East
* South region underperforms significantly
* Sales are concentrated in a few regions → growth opportunity in weaker zones

---

### 📦 Category Analysis

* Technology and Office Supplies deliver the highest profit margins (~17%)
* Furniture generates high sales but extremely low profit margin (~2.5%)

👉 Furniture is a **high-revenue but low-profit category**, indicating pricing or cost inefficiencies

---

### 💸 Discount Impact

* Higher discounts lead to lower profitability
* Some regions show heavy discounting with minimal or negative profit

👉 Discount strategy is a **major factor reducing overall profit**

---

### 👥 Customer Segment Insights

* Consumer segment drives the highest revenue but lower margins
* Corporate and Home Office segments are more profitable

👉 High-volume customers are not necessarily the most profitable

---

### 📈 Sales vs Profit Trend

* Sales show consistent growth over time
* Profit is increasing but not at the same rate

👉 Indicates **inefficiency in scaling profitability**

---

## ⚠️ Core Business Problem

Despite strong sales growth, profitability is impacted due to:

* Excessive discounting
* Low-margin categories (Furniture)
* Inefficient pricing strategies for high-volume customers

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Reduce excessive discounting, especially in low-margin categories
* Focus on high-margin categories like Technology and Office Supplies
* Improve performance in underperforming regions (South)
* Target Corporate customers for better profitability
* Shift focus from **revenue growth → profit optimization**

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* Sales vs Profit analysis by category
* Region-wise performance breakdown
* Profit margin comparison
* Discount vs Profit relationship
* Time-based sales trends
